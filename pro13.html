泰國純天然龍眼蜂蜜

Pure Thai Longyan Flower Honey

生產於泰國北部南奔府的龍眼蜜，100% 無添加額外介質
藉由群體養殖由工蜂群體採集花蜜製成
蜜蜂通過回流和蒸發過程將花蜜轉化為蜂蜜
它們將蜂蜜儲存在蜂箱內的作為原始食物來源

蜂蜜的味道因花蜜的來源而異，而我們也可以提供各種類型和等級的蜂蜜
從養殖、採收至工廠、裝瓶，密封，生產線一氣呵成，不假他人之手

龍眼蜂蜜的好處：
－良好的能量來源，含有多種維生素及礦物質
－有效清除體內自由基
－幫助打造光滑美麗肌膚，為暗沉的秀髮帶來光澤
－幫助癒合傷口

Produced in Lamphun, Northern Thailand, 100% delivered with no additives.
Made by worker bees using nectars from flowers. Honey bees transform nectars into honey by a process of regurgitation and evaporation. Bees store it as their primary food inside beehives.

Flavors of the honey base on the nectar, which we provide various types and grades of honey.
From breeding, harvesting to factories, bottling and sealing. Processes are all done by our own production line.

Benefits of Honey:
－Good source of energy, containing vitamins and minerals
－Effective in removing free radicals from body
－Helps in creating smooth & beautiful skin, gives shine to dull hair
－Helps heal wounds
