油脂分解環保泡沫液

Microbe Oil Decomposition Foam Liquid

德國工藝之天然原液，以微生物、天然植物及生化技術為基底
兼具防焰、降溫、阻熱及分解油汙之效能
此藥劑為中性 pH 值，所以不有害人體、環境並另有清潔效用

此藥劑有別於傳統「AFFF泡沫藥劑」
除了包覆火焰之外更具有分解油酯之效用
不起大顆油泡、不復燃、防打滑，並能有效壓制燃燒之濃煙毒素

有鑑於全球環保意識抬頭，
有別於過去在清理乾粉與油汙的程序上造成河川、下水道及海洋的汙染
此藥劑可直接排放，並且零汙染甚至次分解、再淨化地下道與河川
對於生態綠循環有顯著的功用

German technique based on microbes, natural plants and biochemical technology.
Combining the effectiveness of flame prevention, cooling, heat resistance and oil decomposition
This liquid is neutral pH, so it is not harmful to the human body, the environment and also contains cleaning effects.

This liquid is different from the traditional "AFFF foaming", which can not only coat the flame but also decomposes oil.
With our liquid, it does not produce large oil bubbles, no re-ignition, anti-slippery and it can also effectively suppress toxins of burning smoke.

Due to the rise of global environmental awareness,
Without polluting our rivers, sewers and oceans with traditional fire extinguishers’ dry powder and oil, our agent liquid can be directly disposed.
With zero pollution, our liquid can even re-purification our rivers and sewers.
It is a significant utility for the ecological green cycle.
