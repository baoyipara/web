ABOUT US

寶宜百敦公司自 2009 年開始將台灣的農、工業精品引進到中國及東南亞國家，幾年下來獲得很好的成果並受到相關行業的認同，同時也改善了當地人民的經濟及生活。本公司於台灣的合作對象包括中華民國外交部、財團法人國際合作基金會、經濟部工業局、資訊工業策進會、台灣省體育總會、DHL 等。而我們所代理行銷的產品都有專業生產技術及研發人才做後盾，讓客戶無後顧之憂，在各國均有實際成功的例子。我們定意要開發更多的精品並行銷到全世界，讓更多的人有便利舒適的生活。

我們的產品皆有原廠品質的保證、原廠技術支援，我們亦可 OEM 您的廠牌或是使您成為原廠代理商。本公司亦在 2018 年加入台灣農機同業公會，並多次參與國際推廣、參訪等事宜，若您有好的產品需要推廣，我們可以幫您帶到全世界有需要的地方。


Bao Yi Paraminton Corp. started exporting the best agricultural, industrial products of Taiwan to China and South East Asian countries since 2009. Throughout the years, we have excellent achievements and gained recognition from related industries, improving the economy and living standards of people at the same time. Our company has worked with The Ministry of Foreign Affairs, International Cooperation Development Fund, Industrial Development Bureau, Institute of Information Industry, Taiwan Sports Federation, DHL…etc. All our products have professional manufacturing technology and R&D supporting that customers can rely on, we also have successful examples in multiple countries. We are determined to find the best products and develop them to the world, making a better life for people.

With the assurance, tech-support from the original manufacture, we are able to OEM your brand or make you an agent of the original brand. Our company has became a member of TAMMA Taiwan in 2018, and has been cooperating with partners globally. If you have good products that needs to be seen, allow us to help you bring your goods to the world.
